Our Apps

Four live applications — built with care, launched with purpose, and downloaded by real users.

🕌
Muslim PK – Prayer & Qibla
Lifestyle · Islamic

A simple and reliable Islamic companion app with GPS-based accurate prayer timings and a precise Qibla compass. Also includes Daily Duas, Hadith, Islamic calendar, and Ramadan support — all in a clean, distraction-free interface.

Prayer Times Qibla Compass Daily Duas Ramadan Calendar Privacy First
Download on Play Store
📿
Zikr Pro – Tasbih Counter
Books & Reference · Spiritual

A clean, distraction-free tasbih counter to help you stay consistent with daily zikr. Track streaks, review session history, enjoy optional sound feedback, and count fully offline — no account or login required.

Tasbih Counter Streak Tracking Offline First Sound Feedback Minimal UI
Download on Play Store
📷
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner
Tools · Utilities

A fast, lightweight QR code and barcode scanner. Scan, generate, save, and share QR codes instantly. Supports scan history, dark/light mode, sound & vibration controls — fully offline with zero ads and zero tracking.

QR Scanner Barcode Reader QR Generator Scan History No Ads
Download on Play Store
🍎
Fruit Lens – Fruit Scanner
Food & Drink · AI Powered

AI-powered fruit recognition at your fingertips. Snap a photo or pick from gallery — Fruit Lens instantly identifies the fruit and shows full nutritional data: calories, carbs, protein, fat, and fiber per 100g.

AI Powered Fruit Identifier Nutrition Info No Account Family Safe
Download on Play Store
