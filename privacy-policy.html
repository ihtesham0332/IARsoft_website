Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

At IARsoft, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details provided voluntarily.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, device information, and crash reports to improve our services.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our apps and services.
  • Communicate with you about updates and support.
  • Analyze usage to improve functionality.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers for app functionality (e.g., Firebase for backend services).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us
